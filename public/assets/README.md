# Image assets

Drop the following PNG/JPG/SVG files here before launch. The site references
them at `/assets/<filename>` and will show broken-image icons until you do:

- `huddle-logo.png` — small mark used in the nav, footer, and intro visual
- `hunter.png` — Hunter's team portrait
- `ethan.png` — Ethan's team portrait
- `nate.png` — Nate's team portrait
- `taeeun.png` — Taeeun's team portrait
- `andrew.png` — Andrew's team portrait

Recommended specs:
- Logo: ~64x64 transparent PNG
- Team portraits: 800x800 minimum, square, centered, plain background works best
